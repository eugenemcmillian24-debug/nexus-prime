# Markdown Report Templates

For Morning Brief and Strategy Note only. Weekly Report and Deep Dive use the HTML template at `assets/template.html`.

## Morning Brief

```markdown
# Morning Brief — {date}

## Key Takeaways
- {1-sentence directional call with reasoning}
- {1-sentence directional call with reasoning}
- {1-sentence directional call with reasoning}

## Overnight Recap
{What happened in global markets since last close: US, Europe, Asia, commodities, FX}

## Today's Focus
{2-3 paragraphs on the most market-moving events expected today}

## Data Calendar
| Time | Event | Consensus | Prior |
|------|-------|-----------|-------|

## Risk Watch
{1 paragraph on tail risks or surprises to monitor}
```

## Strategy Note

```markdown
# Market Strategy — {date or period}

## Executive Summary
{Core view on market direction and recommended positioning}

## Macro Regime Assessment
{Where are we in the cycle? Risk-on, risk-off, or transitional?}

## Asset Class Views

### Equities
{Direction, preferred sectors, key names}

### Fixed Income
{Duration view, credit vs rates, specific instruments}

### Commodities
{Directional view with supply-demand reasoning}

### FX
{Key pair views with macro reasoning}

## Thematic Opportunities
### {Theme 1}
{Opportunity description, catalysts, key names}

### {Theme 2}
{Opportunity description, catalysts, key names}

## Risk Matrix
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Hedge |
|------|------------|--------|-------|

## Positioning Summary
| Asset Class | View | Conviction | Action |
|-------------|------|------------|--------|

## Key Dates
{Upcoming catalysts, data releases, events that could shift the view}
```
